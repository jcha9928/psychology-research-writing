레슨 1: 주어와 동사 (Subjects and Actions)

이 레슨은 “문장은 누구에 대한 것인지, 무엇을 했는지를 알려준다”라는 기본 원리를 바탕으로, 주어와 동사를 어떻게 배치하면 독자가 정보를 더 쉽게 찾을 수 있는지 설명합니다. 듀크 대학 자료의 전체 내용을 바탕으로 원칙과 예제를 모두 한국어로 해설하고 영어로 제시합니다.

원칙 1 – 동사에 행동을 담기

동사는 움직임이나 행동을 표현하는 단어입니다. 과학 글쓰기에서 명사화(동사를 명사로 바꾸는 것)를 남용하면 독자가 문장의 행동을 찾기 어려워집니다【147290103937316†L50-L89】. 예를 들어 ‘to analyze’는 명사화하면 ‘analysis’가 되고, “We performed an analysis on the data”와 “We analyzed the data”를 비교하면 후자가 명확합니다. 명사화 자체는 잘못이 아니지만, 추상적 명사로 행동을 숨기는 경우 독자는 불필요한 에너지를 소비합니다.

명사화를 찾고 수정하는 요령: 원고를 읽으며 명사화를 밑줄 치고 동사로 바꿀 수 있는지 검토하거나, 모든 동사를 밑줄 치고 그 동사가 실제 행동을 나타내는지 스스로에게 질문해보세요【147290103937316†L95-L105】. 때로는 전 문장의 행동을 요약하는 명사화가 필요할 수도 있습니다. 예: “We analyzed the data. This analysis demonstrated the need for additional experiments.”

원칙 2 – 등장인물을 주어에 배치하기

문장의 행위자는 주어로 나타나는 것이 자연스럽습니다. ‘movement’ 같은 추상명사가 주어로 오면 누구의 행동인지 파악하기 어렵습니다. 자료에서는 박테리아 이동 예시를 통해 추상 주어를 “The bacteria move themselves…”와 같이 명확한 주어로 바꾸면 이해가 쉬워짐을 보여줍니다【147290103937316†L112-L139】.

문단 전체에서 주어가 일관되게 유지되면 독자가 주제를 따라가기 쉽습니다. 주제를 바꾸려고 의도하지 않는 한 주어가 계속 변하지 않도록 주의하세요. 4문장으로 이루어진 유인원 유전체 예시에서는 첫 번째 버전이 같은 주제를 유지해 이해하기 쉽고, 두 번째 버전은 ‘identification’과 ‘scientists’ 등으로 주어가 계속 바뀌어 혼란스럽습니다【147290103937316†L150-L180】. 또한 여러 주제를 순차적으로 소개해야 하는 경우에는 ‘Technology → DNA sequencing → efficient sequencing…’처럼 논리적 흐름을 따라 주어를 변환하면 됩니다【147290103937316†L190-L201】.

원칙 3 – 주어와 동사를 가깝게 두기

독자는 “누가 무엇을 하는지”를 빠르게 알고 싶습니다. 주어와 동사 사이가 20단어 이상 떨어지면 문장을 다시 읽게 됩니다. 농부 예시에서 주어 Farmers와 동사 are in high demand가 21단어 떨어져 있어 이해가 어려웠지만, “Farmers are in high demand if they can understand…”처럼 주어와 동사를 가깝게 배치하면 명확해집니다【147290103937316†L223-L249】.

긴 목록을 제시할 때도 먼저 문장이 무엇에 관한 것인지 설명하고 목록을 뒤에 놓으세요. “Peanuts, shrimp,… all represent things…”를 “People are commonly allergic to things like peanuts, shrimp…”로 바꾸면 문장이 길어져도 이해하기 쉽습니다【147290103937316†L254-L264】.

예시 및 수정

다음 예제들은 듀크 자료에서 제시한 실제 문장입니다. 원문을 영어로 제공하고, 개선된 문장과 한국어 설명을 덧붙였습니다. 각 예시 아래에 있는 연습 문제에서 직접 수정해 볼 수 있습니다.

예시 1 – ABC 데이터베이스

Original: The ABC database has been subject to different improvements, modifications, and extensions in structure and content over the years.

Explanation (KR): ‘has been subject to’라는 모호한 수동 표현과 명사화들(improvements, modifications, extensions)이 주된 행동을 숨깁니다.

Revised: The curators have improved the structure and content of the ABC database.

예시 2 – 열린 크로마틴 매핑

Original: Mapping of open chromatin regions, post-translational histone modifications and DNA methylation across a whole genome is now feasible, and new non-coding RNAs can be sensitively identified via RNA sequencing.

Explanation (KR): 주어가 긴 명사구이고 동사(is feasible)가 뒤에 있어 문장이 길게 느껴집니다.

Revised: It is now feasible to map open chromatin regions, post‑translational histone modifications and DNA methylation across a whole genome, and to identify new non‑coding RNAs via RNA sequencing.

예시 3 – T‑세포의 상관관계

Original: Significant positive correlations were evident between the substitution rate and a nucleosome score from resting human T‑cells.

Explanation (KR): ‘were evident’라는 빈약한 동사와 명사화(correlation)가 주요 행위를 숨깁니다.

Revised: In resting human T‑cells, the substitution rate correlated with a nucleosome score.

예시 4 – 네번째 예시: 복잡한 주어

Original: The possibility that some termini have a base composition different from that of DNA simply because they are the nearest neighbors of termini specifically recognized by the enzymes can be checked by comparing the experimental results with those expected from the nearest neighbor data.

Explanation (KR): 주어(the possibility…)와 동사(can be checked)가 너무 멀리 떨어져 있으며, 긴 수식어가 주어를 덮고 있습니다.

Revised: If we compare the experimental results with those expected from the nearest neighbor data, we can check the possibility that some termini differ in base composition simply because they neighbor termini recognized by the enzymes.

연습 문제

아래의 영어 문장을 직접 수정해 보세요. 주어와 동사, 등장인물과 행동을 명확히 하도록 노력하세요. ‘Show Answer’를 눌러 모범 답안을 확인할 수 있습니다.

Original: Tyrosine phosphorylation by activated JAKs of cytokine‑receptor cytoplasmic domains then provides binding sites for the Src‑homology‑2 domain of the STAT proteins.

Revised: Activated JAKs phosphorylate cytokine‑receptor cytoplasmic domains on tyrosine, creating binding sites for the STAT proteins’ SH2 domains.

Original: We subjected yeast to 20 min of amino acid deprivation and made ribosome‑footprint and mRNA‑abundance measurements.

Revised: We deprived yeast of amino acids for 20 minutes and then measured ribosome footprints and mRNA abundance.

Original: The assumptions that all sites evolve at one of two evolutionary rates (conserved and nonconserved), that these rates are uniform across the genome, that sites evolve independently conditional on whether they are in conserved or nonconserved regions, and that the phylogenetic models for conserved and nonconserved regions have the same branch‑length proportions, base compositions, and substitution patterns, all represent oversimplifications of the complex process of sequence evolution in eukaryotic genomes.

Revised: These assumptions—that all sites evolve at only two uniform rates across the genome, that sites evolve independently depending on whether they are conserved, and that conserved and nonconserved regions share the same branch lengths, base compositions and substitution patterns—oversimplify the complexity of sequence evolution in eukaryotic genomes.

Original: The number of different mechanisms that may exist for cells to interpret morphogens, and the importance of design features such as feedback or local cell‑cell communication, is unclear.

Revised: We do not yet know how many mechanisms cells use to interpret morphogens or how design features like feedback and local cell–cell communication affect interpretation.

Original: Furthermore, the application of new technologies to further understand the biology of the adipocyte, including location analysis, global DNase hypersensitivity, high‑throughput RNA‑interference screens and computational strategies, promises to enhance our knowledge of this once‑neglected cell.

Revised: Applying new techniques—such as location analysis, genome‑wide DNase hypersensitivity mapping, high‑throughput RNAi screens and computational methods—will enhance our understanding of adipocyte biology.

Original: Indeed, in the mouse, the sequences of large noncoding RNAs, which probably have no 3′ polyA tail, were reconstructed from the fragments of truncated cDNAs.

Revised: Researchers reconstructed the sequences of large noncoding RNAs in mouse from fragments of truncated cDNAs, even though these RNAs likely lack a 3′ polyA tail.

Original: Localized fluctuations in substitution rate are widely employed to draw inference concerning the phenotypic significance of genomic sequence.

Revised: Researchers often infer the phenotypic significance of genomic sequences by examining localized fluctuations in substitution rates.

Original: A number of promoters exhibited significant positive correlations between the footprinting estimated distribution of K and nucleosome score estimated from T‑cells.

Revised: Several promoters showed strong positive correlations between the footprinting‑based distribution of K and nucleosome scores estimated from T cells.

Original: In this study, we subjected seven different primate species to comparative analysis of the radial distribution pattern of human chromosome 18‑ and 19‑homologous chromatin by three‑dimensional fluorescence in situ hybridization.

Revised: In this study we used three‑dimensional fluorescence in situ hybridization to compare how the homologous chromatin of human chromosomes 18 and 19 is radially distributed in seven primate species.

Original: An analysis of functional connectivity in the default mode network was conducted using resting‑state fMRI.

Revised: We used resting‑state fMRI to analyze functional connectivity in the default mode network.

Original: A total of 30 participants were subjected to a battery of neuropsychological tests to assess executive function.

Revised: We assessed executive function in 30 participants using a battery of neuropsychological tests.

Original: The effect of stress on amygdala activation was measured by the team.

Revised: The team measured how stress affects amygdala activation.

Original: Significant positive correlations were evident between the substitution rate and a nucleosome score from resting human T‑cells.

Revised: In resting human T‑cells, the substitution rate correlated with a nucleosome score.

Original: The possibility that some termini have a base composition different from that of DNA simply because they are the nearest neighbors of termini specifically recognized by the enzymes can be checked by comparing the experimental results with those expected from the nearest neighbor data.

Revised: If we compare the experimental results with those expected from the nearest neighbor data, we can check the possibility that some termini differ in base composition simply because they neighbor termini recognized by the enzymes.
